Welcome to Keywords Hatch!!!

This is a keyword tool with which you could easily format your keywords the way you want. Although this tool is originally intended for Amazon keywords formatting, you could also use it for other platforms as longs as the format is right.

At this moment only Instashaper and Ad Phrases are available. They both filter the keywords you input by deleting most of the brands and re-arrange them into groups of 1000, 500 or other number you choose characters so that you could easily put them in your search terms. You don't have to worry about case issues as this tool automatically transform all input words to lower case to match the brands. The result generated by Instashaper are individual non-redundant word while Ad Phrases's result are phrases with repeated words.

More features will be added soon!!!
